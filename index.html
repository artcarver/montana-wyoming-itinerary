Before You Go — Reservations
Hotels are all booked. These are the flights, car, activities and passes left to lock in — with who to book and by when.
Book Flights
Round-trip San Diego (SAN) → Bozeman (BZN), Delta with a connection. Out Aug 8 (6:29 AM departure), home Aug 16. Book early — August fares to BZN climb fast and seats are limited.
Book ASAP
Book Rental Car
Pick up and drop off at Bozeman (BZN), Aug 8–16. An SUV or AWD is worth it for the mountain passes and gravel pullouts. Counters are right in the terminal — reserve ahead, as BZN inventory sells out in peak summer.
Book ASAP
Snake River Scenic Float
Solitude Float Trips (highest-rated) · 307-733-2871 · grand-teton-scenic-floats.com. Aim for the 5 PM Aug 14 slot. Backup: Barker-Ewing, barkerewing.com.
Lock in by Aug 1
Dinner Reservations
Two nights worth booking on OpenTable: Aug 14, Jackson — Local (steakhouse on the Town Square) or Café Genevieve; an 8 PM table pairs with the float. Aug 15, Bozeman (a Saturday) — Blackbird Kitchen or Montana Ale Works. Snake River Brewing & Hatch in Jackson are walk-up.
Book ~1 week out
Park Entry Pass
America the Beautiful annual pass, $80 · store.usgs.gov. Covers Glacier, Yellowstone & Grand Teton for the whole car. No vehicle reservations needed anywhere in 2026.
Anytime / at gate
Jenny Lake Boat Shuttle
Jenny Lake Boating · jennylakeboating.com. No reservation — boats run every 10–15 min, 7 AM–7 PM. Just arrive early (~8 AM) at the East Boat Dock and pay.
Walk-up · no booking
Many Glacier Boat Tour
Glacier Park Boat Co. · glacierparkboats.com. The Aug 10, 5 PM tour is already reserved — nothing more to do but show up by 4:30.
Reserved ✓
End of the trail · Fly home Aug 16
